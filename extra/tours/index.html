Tournaments

This page gives a rough overview of the tournaments that RL has hosted.

Game Tournaments Description
Misc 4 Man Locals (Mini Tournaments) Events hosted by RL where miscellaneous games are played depending on what everyone feels like. Most of the time it's Melee or Project Meme.
Joy Mech Fight Jibber's Cribb One of the main tournament series for Joy Mech Fight, held online. This tournament series had a rotating time table for each iteration, meaning that one tournament entry would be inclusive to Europe, and another entry would be more inclusive to Japan and Oceania.
Joy Mech Fight Now We All Fight An online tournament series in a ladder format where the highest placers in the ladder would end up in a top 8 bracket. This tournament series was created to let all players in the global JMF scene compete in the same event.
Joy Mech Fight Clear the Skies An online tournament for Joy Mech Fight where the rules were experimented with to help balance infinite combos in the game. After the tournament, it seemed apparent that the community preferred the rules as they previously were.
